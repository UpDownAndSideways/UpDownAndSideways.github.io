About Ame's Skin Therapy

Ame's Skin Therapy was founded by Amanda, a Master Aesthetician with over 20 years of hands-on experience helping clients achieve healthy, glowing skin. Licensed in Texas and holding the prestigious CIDESCO and CIBTAC international certifications, Amanda brings both global knowledge and local care to every treatment.

Throughout her career, Amanda has traveled across the U.S. as an educator and mentor, sharing her passion for advanced skincare and empowering other estheticians to elevate their craft. Her approach is all about results with relaxation — blending clinical expertise with a warm, personalized touch.
